About CodeQuestt

CodeQuestt is an annual hackathon designed for students in Grades 8 to 12, aiming to foster creativity, problem-solving, and technical skills. Whether you're a beginner or a seasoned coder, CodeQuestt provides an opportunity to work in teams or solo to tackle innovative challenges.

Featured Events

Code Clash

A competitive programming challenge where coders battle head-to-head to solve the toughest problems.

Market Mavericks

Pitch your ideas and compete in a startup simulation, where innovation meets business acumen.

Chess Titans

A strategic chess competition, testing your brainpower and decision-making under pressure.

Artistic Odyssey

Express your creativity through digital design and artistic ventures, showcasing the blend of art and code.
